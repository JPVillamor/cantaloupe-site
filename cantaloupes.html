About Us

WE are a national organization dedicated to achieving the ultimate goal of eradicating #AllCantaloupes. Our society was created in 2017 with the inception of this very webpage. Our group is the long awaited answer to the call for justice against the very embodiment of tyrannical oppression: the fruit known as the cantaloupe.

The ACSA's Founder had heard the cries of many who believed that the cantaloupe was a symbol of intolerance and bigotry, and in his abundance of wisdom utilized the resources at his disposal to create a safe haven for like-minded patrons of the anti-cantaloupe school of thought. In the early days of the ACSA, its constituents referred to themselves as "Canta-Nopes". As you can see, this was a clever play on the word "cantaloupes"; thus, following the same logic, a singular one of these would be referred to as a "Canta-Nope".
